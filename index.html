Reliable lawn service

Kimbal's offers week after week lawn cutting with a rotational cutter, using sharp, clean blades that will be adjusted so that you get the best cut for your grass write. Our lawn cutting services ordinarily start in March and proceed through November, or until the point that seasonal conditions make it unsafe for your lawn to be cut. Your Kimbal Lawn Care Pro will decide the best cutting tallness for your lawn based on grass write and seasonal conditions. Your Pro will also alter cutting course on a week after week basis to avoid ruts in your lawn.
